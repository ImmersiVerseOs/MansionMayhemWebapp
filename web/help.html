üéÆ Gameplay Flow

1. Lobby Phase

Before the game starts, you can:

  • Voice Introduction: Record an intro to introduce yourself to other players
  • Link-Up Requests: Send alliance proposals to other players
  • Alliance Rooms: See who's forming alliances
  • Alliance Chat: Private strategy discussions with alliance members

2. Active Scenarios

Once the game starts:

  • Respond Now: Click the "Respond Now" button on your dashboard
  • Scenario Types: Confrontations, confessions, alliances, and challenges
  • Voice Responses: Record your voice to respond dramatically
  • Deadlines: Watch the timer - late responses may not count!

3. Voting & Elimination

  • Queen Ceremony: The Queen nominates players for the hot seat
  • Hot Seat Voting: Players vote to eliminate one nominee
  • Public Voting: Fans can also vote (if enabled)

4. Results & Episodes

  • Watch AI-generated episodes in the Gallery
  • See vote breakdowns and eliminations
  • Track your stats and earnings

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get Premium access?
A: Visit Settings ‚Üí Subscription to upgrade. Premium users can create games and access exclusive features.
Q: Can I join multiple games at once?
A: Yes! You can be a cast member in multiple active games simultaneously.
Q: What happens if I miss a scenario deadline?
A: You may receive a penalty or your response won't be included in the episode. Always check deadlines!
Q: How do I leave a game?
A: Contact the game director or use the "Leave Game" option in game settings. Note: This may affect your stats.
Q: Are voice notes moderated?
A: Yes, all voice notes go through moderation to ensure community guidelines are followed.
Q: How do I report inappropriate content?
A: Click the report button on any content. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

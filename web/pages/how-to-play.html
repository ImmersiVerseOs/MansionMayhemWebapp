‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

üíµ How much money can I make?
It varies based on activity level and engagement. Most cast members earn $500-$2,000/month. Top performers with high drama and screen time can earn $3,000-$5,000+. You're paid monthly via Stripe.
‚è±Ô∏è How much time do I need to commit?
Depends on your gameplay mode! Manual: 5-10 hours/week. Hybrid: 2-5 hours/week. Full Control: 30 minutes/week just to watch episodes. You can always adjust your mode in settings.
üìÖ How often are scenarios posted?
Typically 3-5 scenarios per week. Some are urgent (2-4 hour deadline), others give you 12-24 hours to respond. In Hybrid/Full Control mode, AI can handle urgent ones while you're offline.
üé¨ When do episodes come out?
New episodes publish every week, usually on Fridays. Each episode is 15-30 minutes and features the best drama from that week's scenarios. You'll get notified when you're featured.
üë• Can I form alliances with other cast members?
Absolutely! Alliances, friendships, and rivalries make the show interesting. You can coordinate responses with allies, though keep in mind that betrayals make for great TV (and more screen time!).
üö™ Can I quit anytime?
Yes! There's no long-term commitment. You can pause or leave at any time. If you leave, you'll continue earning from episodes you're already in, but won't receive new scenarios.

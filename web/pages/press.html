Company Overview

What is Mansion Mayhem?

Mansion Mayhem is the world's first AI-native interactive reality TV platform. We enable anyone to become a cast member in a reality show where their gameplay decisions are automatically transformed into professional video episodes using cutting-edge AI video generation technology.

How It Works

Cast members respond to daily scenarios with multiple-choice decisions and optional voice notes. When they can't respond in time, our personality-based AI makes decisions for them based on learned behavioral patterns. All responses are then automatically converted into episodic video content featuring either users' actual faces (via FaceCast) or custom AI characters.

Key Innovation

Our patent-pending multi-mode gameplay system solves the core problem of reality TV production: participant dropout due to time constraints. With Hybrid Mode, users get a 4-hour grace period before AI takes over, ensuring 100% participation rates while maintaining authentic human decision-making when users are available.

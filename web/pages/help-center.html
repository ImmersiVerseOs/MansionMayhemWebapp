Popular Questions

How much money can I realistically make? ‚ñº
Most cast members earn between $500-$2,000 per month depending on activity level and engagement. Top performers with high drama and screen time can earn $3,000-$5,000+. Your earnings depend on screen time, viewer engagement, tips, and the quality of your responses.
How does FaceCast work? ‚ñº
FaceCast uses AI to create a digital representation of your face from 5-20 photos you upload. This allows us to generate realistic videos of your character without requiring you to film anything. Your photos are encrypted and stored securely, and we never use your likeness outside of Mansion Mayhem.
Can I change my gameplay mode later? ‚ñº
Yes! You can switch between Manual Control, Hybrid Mode, and Full Control anytime in your settings. Try different modes to find what works best for your schedule and play style.
How often are episodes published? ‚ñº
New episodes are published weekly, typically on Fridays. Each episode is 15-30 minutes and features the best drama from that week's scenarios. You'll get notified when you appear in an episode.
What if I want to quit? ‚ñº
You can leave at any time with no penalties. You'll continue earning from episodes you're already in, but won't receive new scenarios. Your FaceCast and data are deleted within 30 days of account closure.

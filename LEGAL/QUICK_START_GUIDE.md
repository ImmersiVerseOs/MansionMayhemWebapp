## üéØ Your Three Options

### Option 1: Free Trade Secret Protection (Do This Today)
**Cost:** $0
**Time:** 30 minutes

**What to do:**
1. Copy `TRADE_SECRET_NOTICE.md` to your repository root (already there)
2. Make your GitHub repo private:
   ```bash
   # Via GitHub website:
   Settings > Danger Zone > Change visibility > Make private
   ```
3. Require anyone with repo access to sign `NDA_TEMPLATE.md`

**Result:** Your code is legally protected as trade secrets indefinitely (like Coca-Cola formula)

---

### Option 2: DIY Provisional Patents (Budget Option)
**Cost:** ~$300-$400
**Time:** 1-2 days of your time

**What to do:**
1. Review the two provisional patent documents I created
2. Create simple diagrams (can be hand-drawn sketches):
   - System architecture diagrams
   - Flowcharts showing how the systems work
3. Register at USPTO.gov
4. File both applications online (EFS-Web system)
5. Pay $150 per application (small entity rate)

**Result:**
- ‚úÖ "Patent Pending" status immediately
- ‚úÖ 12-month priority date (first to file)
- ‚úÖ Time to refine before full patent
- ‚è∞ Must convert to full patent within 12 months (~$20k-$40k)

**USPTO Filing:** https://www.uspto.gov/patents/basics/patent-process

---

### Option 3: Patent Attorney (Recommended for Investors/Acquisition)
**Cost:** ~$4,000-$10,000 upfront, ~$20,000-$40,000 in 12 months
**Time:** 2-3 weeks with attorney

**What to do:**
1. Find patent attorney specializing in software/AI:
   - Michigan State Bar referral: https://www.michbar.org/
   - USPTO Attorney Search: https://oedci.uspto.gov/OEDCI/
2. Share the provisional patent documents I created
3. Attorney refines claims and files on your behalf

**Result:**
- ‚úÖ Professional legal review
- ‚úÖ Stronger patent foundation
- ‚úÖ Better chance of USPTO approval
- ‚úÖ Guided through full patent process
- ‚úÖ Strong IP position for investors
